Homepage Rethink Directions

Goal: make the app understandable at a glance without adding a tutorial or a big new flow.

Option A · Explore First
Reality Archive | Explore | My Places | Museum
Live map dominates the first screen
Nearby place card
Saved memory preview

A. Explore First

The homepage immediately feels like a map product. Best if discovery is the true main action.

Pros

  • Fastest to understand
  • Strong product identity
  • Makes “what do I do?” obvious

Cons

  • Home and Explore become very close
  • Slightly bigger homepage change
Option B · Preview Then Enter
Reality Archive | Explore | My Places | Museum

Explore nearby places and turn them into memories.

A cleaner homepage that shows the product clearly without turning Home into the full map screen.

Strong map + place preview panel
1. Discover
2. Save memory
3. View museum

B. Preview Then Enter

Recommended. The homepage explains the product through visual hierarchy, then pushes one clear action into Explore.

Pros

  • Keeps Home and Explore distinct
  • Very understandable
  • Moderate implementation size

Cons

  • Less bold than a map-first homepage
Option C · Archive + Museum Split
Reality Archive | Explore | My Places | Museum
Archive side: places and memories
Museum side: AI exhibit preview

C. Archive + Museum Split

Explains both halves of the product, but it risks keeping the user unsure about which action matters most.

Pros

  • Shows the full idea
  • Feels richer and more editorial

Cons

  • Still more ambiguous
  • Weaker primary action
